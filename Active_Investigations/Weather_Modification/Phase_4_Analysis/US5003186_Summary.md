# US5003186A Investigation Summary
**Hughes Aircraft / Raytheon Stratospheric Welsbach Seeding Patent**

## Quick Reference

**Patent:** US5003186A  
**Filed:** April 23, 1990  
**Granted:** March 26, 1991  
**Expired:** April 23, 2010  
**Original Assignee:** Hughes Aircraft Company  
**Current Owner:** Raytheon Company (since 2004)

## Key Findings

### What It IS
✅ **Authentic geoengineering patent** describing stratospheric aerosol injection  
✅ **Proposes aluminum oxide (Al₂O₃) and thorium oxide (ThO₂)** as reflective particles  
✅ **Aircraft-based dispersal** at 7-13 km altitude (flight levels)  
✅ **Created by legitimate scientists** (Chang & Shih - optical/radiation physicists)  
✅ **Defense contractor involvement** (Hughes later acquired by Raytheon)

### What It IS NOT
❌ **No deployment evidence** found after exhaustive search  
❌ **Not operationally implemented** by Hughes or Raytheon  
❌ **Scientifically flawed** - particles 50-500x too large (10-100 microns vs optimal 0.2-0.3 microns)  
❌ **Superseded by better approach** - sulfates proven more effective after Mt. Pinatubo  
❌ **Not detected in atmosphere** - NOAA measured 20,000+ stratospheric particles, no signature found

## Why Approach Failed

1. **Particle size wrong** - 50-500x too large, sediment too fast
2. **Scattering inefficient** - larger particles less effective per kg
3. **Stratospheric heating** - metal oxides heat stratosphere more than sulfates
4. **No natural analog** - couldn't validate with volcanic data
5. **Thorium radioactive** - regulatory nightmare, health concerns
6. **More expensive** - aluminum oxide costs more per unit cooling than sulfur

## Atmospheric Measurements

**NOAA/CIRES Studies (2010-2023):**
- **20,000+ particles analyzed** with mass spectrometry
- **Composition found:** 90%+ sulfuric acid + water
- **Aluminum sources:** 70% spacecraft reentry + 30% meteoroids (natural)
- **Total aluminum flux:** ~230 tons/year (baseline natural level)
- **Hughes deployment would add:** 1,000-10,000 tons/year (5-50x increase)
- **Actual detection:** NO INCREASE beyond natural sources

**Verdict:** If deployed at meaningful scale, would be unambiguously detected. It has not been.
