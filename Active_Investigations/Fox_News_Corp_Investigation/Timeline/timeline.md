# Fox News Corporation Timeline

## Complete Chronological History

### 1996-2000: Founding Era

**October 7, 1996** - Fox News Channel launches under Roger Ailes direction
- Rupert Murdoch provides backing and strategic vision
- Initial positioning as conservative alternative to CNN

**2002** - Fox News surpasses CNN to become #1 cable news network
- Marks beginning of 23-year dominance period
- Post-9/11 coverage establishes market position

### 2004-2015: Consolidation Period

**September 2004** - First Bill O'Reilly harassment settlement ($9M)
- Early sign of workplace culture issues
- Settlements kept confidential

### 2016-2017: Crisis and Transition

**July 6, 2016** - Gretchen Carlson files sexual harassment lawsuit against Roger Ailes
- Catalyzes broader investigation
- Multiple women come forward

**July 21, 2016** - Roger Ailes resigns as CEO
- Rupert Murdoch assumes interim CEO role
- End of founding era

**September 2016** - Carlson settlement finalized ($20M)

**April 19, 2017** - Bill O'Reilly terminated
- $13M+ in settlements to multiple accusers disclosed
- Major primetime loss

**May 17, 2017** - Roger Ailes dies at age 77

**May 16-23, 2017** - Seth Rich conspiracy story published and retracted
- Fox News publishes false story linking DNC staffer death to email leaks
- Retracted after public outcry
- Parents later settle defamation suit (seven figures)

**August 2017** - Fox News pulled from UK broadcast
- Ofcom violations of UK Broadcasting Code cited
- Network claims commercial reasons

### 2018-2019: New Structure

**May 18, 2018** - Suzanne Scott named CEO of Fox News Media
- First woman in role
- Post-Ailes reorganization complete

**March 19, 2019** - Fox Corporation formally created
- Disney acquires 21st Century Fox assets for $71.3B
- Fox retains news broadcasting and sports
- Sky sold separately to Comcast (~$15B)

### 2020-2021: Election and Lawsuits

**March 2020** - Tubi streaming service acquired ($440M)

**November 2020** - Seth Rich family settlement (seven figures)

**February 2021** - Smartmatic files $2.7B defamation lawsuit
- Based on 2020 election coverage

**March 26, 2021** - Dominion Voting Systems files $1.6B lawsuit
- Alleges false statements about voting machines
- Discovery reveals extensive internal communications

### 2023: Reckoning Year

**March 31, 2023** - Judge rules Dominion's case has merit
- Finds Fox statements "crystal clear" false
- Sets stage for trial

**April 18, 2023** - Fox settles with Dominion for $787.5M
- Largest known media defamation settlement in U.S. history
- No apology or admission of wrongdoing

**April 24, 2023** - Tucker Carlson terminated
- Decided by Lachlan Murdoch and Suzanne Scott
- One week after Dominion settlement
- Internal messages from discovery cited as factor

**September 21, 2023** - Rupert Murdoch announces retirement
- Steps down as Chairman of Fox Corporation and News Corp
- Becomes Chairman Emeritus

**November 2023** - Lachlan Murdoch becomes sole Chairman
- Consolidates control over both companies
- Rupert remains advisory

### 2024: Succession Battle

**December 9, 2024** - Nevada probate commissioner rules against Murdoch trust amendment
- 96-page decision characterizes effort as "carefully crafted charade"
- Finds Rupert and Lachlan acted in "bad faith"
- Attempt to remove James, Elisabeth, and Prudence from trust fails

### 2025: Control Secured

**January 2025** - Multiple regulatory victories
- FCC dismisses petition to deny Fox 29 Philadelphia license renewal
- Appeals court rejects Fox's dismissal motion in Smartmatic case

**September 8, 2025** - Murdoch family reaches succession settlement
- Lachlan gains sole voting control through new trust (LGC Holdco)
- Control secured through 2050
- James Elisabeth and Prudence exit trust
- Each sibling receives ~$1.1B from sale of 16.9M Class B shares
- Total buyout: $3.3B

**November 2025** - Palantir partnership disclosed
- AI newsroom tools partnership revealed
- Palantir engineers embedded in Fox newsroom for ~1 year
- Represents direct commercial tie to major defense contractor
