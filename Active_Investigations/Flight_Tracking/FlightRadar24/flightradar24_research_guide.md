## Technical Jargon Decoder (For Your Audience)

- **ADS-B**: Like an airplane's public Twitter account, broadcasting position constantly
- **MLAT**: Triangulation - like finding a phone by pinging cell towers
- **Transponder**: The transmitter on planes that broadcasts their data
- **ICAO Code**: Airplane's permanent ID number (like a VIN for cars)
- **Tail Number**: Airplane's registration number (can change)
- **Mode S**: Older transponder technology (still trackable)
- **LADD**: FAA's "please don't show my plane" program
- **PIA**: Temporary fake airplane ID for privacy
- **1090 MHz**: The radio frequency planes broadcast on
- **Squawk**: Transponder code for different situations
